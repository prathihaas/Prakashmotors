## Why New Holland's Higher Hydraulics Make a Difference for Implements

New Holland tractors — especially the 3630 TX Plus (1700 kg) and 4710 Excel (1700 kg) — have the highest hydraulic lift capacity in their HP segments. This matters because:

- Heavier implements (heavy disc ploughs, laser levellers) can be attached without straining the hydraulic pump
- Consistent hydraulic pressure ensures implement depth stays constant throughout the field
- Higher lift height allows easier transport position without implement dragging on uneven ground

Competitor tractors at the same HP often max out at 1400–1500 kg lift, limiting the implements they can safely carry.

## Complete Implement Guide for Nizamabad Farmers

### 1. Rotavator — Essential for Paddy Fields

The rotavator is the most widely used tractor implement in Nizamabad's paddy belt. It combines tillage and field preparation in a single pass, reducing field preparation time by 40% versus disc plough + cultivator combination.

| Specification | Details |
|---|---|
| Minimum HP required | 35 HP (NH 3230 TX) |
| Recommended HP | 45–50 HP for heavy clay soils |
| Working width | 1.2m (35HP), 1.5m (47HP), 1.8m (50HP+) |
| Price range | ₹55,000–90,000 |
| Best use season | Pre-kharif (May–June), post-harvest (October) |
| Fuel per acre (NH 3630) | 3.2–3.8 L |

**Brands available at Prakash Motors**: Fieldking, Shaktiman, NH-compatible branded rotavators

**Nizamabad usage**: Rotavator is the primary tool for paddy field preparation in Armoor and Bodhan mandals. Ideal for incorporating the previous crop stubble while preparing a fine seedbed.

### 2. Disc Plough — King of Black Cotton Soil

For Nizamabad's deep Vertisol (black cotton) soils, the disc plough provides the deep primary tillage that breaks the hardpan and ensures good root penetration. No other implement can replace it for cotton field preparation.

| Specification | Details |
|---|---|
| Minimum HP required | 45 HP (for 3-disc) |
| Recommended HP | 47–55 HP (NH 3630 or NH 6010) |
| Disc sizes | 20", 22", 24" diameter |
| Working depth | 18–25 cm |
| Price range (3-disc) | ₹45,000–75,000 |
| Best use season | Pre-kharif (May), pre-rabi (October–November) |
| Fuel per acre (NH 3630) | 4.0–5.5 L (depth-dependent) |

**NH advantage**: The 3630 TX Plus's 1700 kg hydraulic lift ensures the 3-disc plough maintains consistent depth through sticky, expanding Vertisol clay that tends to suck implements down.

For sugarcane and deep-rooted crops in Nizamabad's Yellareddy and Nandipet mandals, the MB plough provides the deepest tillage — 30–35 cm — turning soil completely for new crop establishment.

| Specification | Details |
|---|---|
| Minimum HP required | 50 HP (NH 4710 Excel) |
| Working depth | 25–35 cm |
| Number of bottoms | 2 (most common in Nizamabad) |
| Price range | ₹35,000–55,000 |
| Best use season | Pre-sugarcane sowing (October–November) |
| Fuel per acre | 5.5–7.0 L |

MB plough use is declining as subsoil ploughing becomes more common, but it remains essential for sugarcane fields in deep black soil areas.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Can I use Mahindra implements on my New Holland tractor?**
Most implements use standard 3-point linkage and PTO specifications (Category I and II), making them cross-compatible. However, for optimal performance and warranty compliance, Prakash Motors recommends implements sized to your specific NH model's lift capacity and PTO HP. We can advise on compatibility for specific implements.

**Q2: What is the most important implement for a new Nizamabad farmer to buy first?**
For paddy farmers: rotavator. For cotton farmers: disc plough. For those doing both crops: start with a rotavator and rent a disc plough for the first season while you assess your budget. Prakash Motors can help you plan a phased implement investment strategy.

**Q3: Are finance schemes available for implement purchases?**
Yes. Several banks and NH Finance offer combined tractor-and-implement loans, or separate implement loans for existing tractor owners. The Agricultural Mechanisation Mission also provides subsidy on selected implements (rotavator, transplanter, laser leveller). Ask our team for current scheme details.

**Q4: How long do disc plough blades last in Nizamabad's black cotton soil?**
Vertisol soils are abrasive and wear disc blades faster than sandy soils. Expect 200–300 acres before blade replacement is needed. Quality blades (hardened steel) last 15–20% longer and are worth the premium. Prakash Motors stocks genuine replacement blades at competitive prices.
