## Why Choose an Authorized Dealer?

This is a question worth answering directly — especially in a market where unauthorized dealers and grey-market equipment exist.

### 1. Genuine Products with Full Warranty
Tractors purchased from Prakash Motors come with:
- New Holland's official 2-year / 2000-hour warranty
- Warranty honored across New Holland's national network
- No warranty on grey-market or parallel import equipment

### 2. Trained Technicians
Our service team is trained by New Holland. This means:
- They understand the specific engineering of each model
- Diagnostic tools are proper NH diagnostic equipment
- Service procedures follow NH specifications exactly
- Turbocharged engines, TREM IV emission systems, and advanced hydraulics require specialist knowledge

An untrained mechanic "fixing" your TREM IV engine or advanced hydraulic system can cause expensive damage.

### 3. Genuine Spare Parts
We stock genuine New Holland spare parts at our Nizamabad warehouse:
- Filters (oil, fuel, air, hydraulic)
- Belts (fan, alternator)
- Clutch components
- Hydraulic pump and valve components
- Engine components for emergency repairs

Counterfeit and sub-standard parts are widespread in India's used parts market. Sub-standard parts fail faster and can damage other systems. Genuine parts maintain your tractor's performance and warranty.

### 4. Finance and Subsidy Support
Authorized dealers have established relationships with banks for agricultural loans. Our team assists you with:
- Bank loan applications
- Government subsidy applications (SMAM, PM Kisan Tractor Yojana)
- Document preparation

### 5. Local Knowledge and Trusted Advice
We operate in Nizamabad district. We know the soil types in Banswada vs Yellareddy. We understand the turmeric season demands in Armoor. We know what paddy farmers in Nizamsagar mandal need. Our recommendations are based on local knowledge, not generic national selling scripts.
