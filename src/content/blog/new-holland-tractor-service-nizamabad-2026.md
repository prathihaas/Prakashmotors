## Genuine Parts — Why It Matters

Some farmers try to save money using local market parts. This is a false economy. Here is why genuine NH parts are non-negotiable:

- **Precision fit**: NH engines and hydraulics are engineered to tight tolerances. Non-genuine oil filters can allow particles into the oil circuit, causing premature engine wear.
- **Warranty protection**: Using non-genuine parts voids your 2-year/2000-hour warranty immediately. A ₹300 saving on an oil filter can cost you ₹80,000 in an uncovered engine repair.
- **Fuel system integrity**: Genuine fuel filters prevent injector fouling, which is the leading cause of fuel efficiency drop in tractors over 500 hours.

Prakash Motors stocks the full range of fast-moving genuine NH parts — oil filters, air filters, fuel filters, belts, batteries, seals, and hydraulic components — with same-day availability in most cases.

**Q1: How often should I service my New Holland tractor?**
New Holland recommends service every 250 operating hours or 6 months, whichever comes first. For Nizamabad farmers running 400–600 hours per year, this means 2 services per year. Skipping even one service can reduce engine life by 20–30%.
