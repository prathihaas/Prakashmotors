## Simba 20 (55HP) — In-Depth Performance

### Engine Character

The 4-cylinder 55HP turbocharged engine in the Simba 20 is notably different from the 3-cylinder engines in the NH 3600-2 Excel. The 4-cylinder configuration delivers:
- Smoother power delivery across all RPM ranges
- Better torque at low RPM — critical for heavy implements in Nizamabad's clay soils
- Lower vibration for operator comfort
- Better thermal management in extreme heat conditions

For Telangana's summer operations (March–June) when temperatures exceed 40°C, the 4-cylinder engine maintains more consistent power output.

### 12-Speed Gearbox

The 12F + 3R gearbox is a significant advantage over basic 8-speed tractors:
- Multiple field working speeds for different operations and soil conditions
- True creep speeds for precision operations (transplanting, seeding)
- High transport speeds for road operation between fields
- Smooth gear engagement reduces operator fatigue

For a farmer managing paddy, cotton, and turmeric across different fields and mandals, this flexibility is operationally valuable.

### Hydraulic Performance

2200 kg lifting capacity with 55 L/min flow enables:
- 8-ft rotavator operation
- Heavy disc ploughs in any condition
- Precision depth control for turmeric bed formation
- Standard implements with reserve capacity for challenging conditions

### Fuel Economy

At 55HP, expect:
- Light cultivation: 3.0–3.5 L/hour
- Rotavation (full load): 4.0–5.0 L/hour
- Transport: 2.5–3.0 L/hour
- Deep tillage: 4.5–6.0 L/hour

The 70-litre tank gives a comfortable operational range without mid-field refueling for most operations.

## Simba 30 (75HP) — In-Depth Performance

### Where 75HP Makes a Difference

The NH 5620 TX Plus TREM IV is also 75HP in the standard NH range. The Simba 30's differentiator at 75HP is primarily:
- Higher specification hydraulics (3000 kg vs standard)
- Enhanced operator features
- Better build finish and materials
- 4WD standard configuration

For Telangana farmers, 75HP is relevant when:
- Operating above 50 acres with multiple large implements
- Running custom hiring services requiring maximum versatility
- Large sugarcane operations needing deep sub-soiling
- Commercial operations where time efficiency is critical

### Heavy Operations Capability

With 3000 kg hydraulic capacity and 70 L/min flow, the Simba 30 can handle:
- 9–10 ft rotavators
- 5–7 tyne sub-soilers
- Heavy disc harrows (24+ disc)
- Large paddy transplanters
- Precision seeding equipment

For the emerging precision agriculture segment in Telangana, the Simba 30's hydraulic specifications are future-ready.

### 4WD Performance in Telangana

Black cotton soil in Banswada, Balkonda, and Bodhan areas is notoriously difficult in wet conditions. The Simba 30 with 4WD delivers:
- Dramatically better traction in monsoon-wet paddy fields
- Reduced wheel slip during heavy tillage — less soil disturbance, better fuel efficiency
- Stability on slopes in Yellareddy and Nizamsagar areas
- Better headland turning without track damage in wet soil
