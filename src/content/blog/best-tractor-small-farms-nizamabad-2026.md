## Why 35HP is the Sweet Spot for 5–15 Acre Farms

Many small farmers are tempted to buy 47–50HP "for future growth." This is usually a mistake for 5–15 acre farms. Here is why 35HP makes more financial sense:

| Factor | 35HP (NH 3230 TX) | 47HP (NH 3630 TX Plus) | Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Purchase price | ₹6.5–7 L | ₹8–8.5 L | Save ₹1.5 L upfront |
| Monthly EMI (60 months) | ₹10,500–11,500 | ₹14,800–15,500 | Save ₹4,000/month |
| Fuel consumption | 3.2 L/hr | 3.9 L/hr | Save ₹65/hr = ₹16,500/yr at 300 hr |
| Weight (field compaction) | Lower | Higher | Less soil damage on small plots |
| Manoeuvrability | Better | Good | Easier on small plots with tight headlands |

For a 10-acre farm doing 300 hours/year, the 47HP model wastes ₹1.5 lakh more in EMIs plus ₹16,500 more in fuel per year — for no operational benefit that a 35HP cannot provide.

## NH 3230 TX Field Performance in Nizamabad Conditions

### Paddy Operations (Kharif)

The NH 3230 TX handles all standard paddy operations efficiently on farms up to 15 acres:

- **Rotavation**: 1.2m rotavator, 1 pass per acre, 2.8–3.2 L/acre fuel
- **Puddling**: Comfortable at standard depth in Nizamabad alluvial paddy soils
- **Paddy transplanter**: Full PTO compatibility, 0.35–0.45 acres/hour capacity
- **Bund repair**: Easy with front blade attachment

For very deep black cotton paddy soils in Armoor mandal (where sticky Vertisol is common), the 3230 TX may occasionally struggle with heavy 3-disc ploughs. For these conditions, a 2-disc plough is recommended.

### Cotton Operations (Rabi)

- **Ridger**: Excellent — NH 3230's 1500 kg hydraulics manage ridging cleanly
- **Cultivator (7-tyne)**: Smooth inter-row cultivation at 4–5 km/hr
- **Disc plough (2-disc)**: Comfortable — 3-disc is marginal in very heavy clay
- **Transport**: 3-tonne trailer load at 25–30 km/hr road speed

## Addressing the EMI Concern — The Math That Makes It Work

The most common concern from Nizamabad small farmers: "₹10,500–11,000/month is too high for my income."

Here is how successful small-farm owners in the district solve this equation:

### The Custom Hiring Model

Custom hiring — operating your tractor for neighbours' farms at market rates — is the most effective EMI offset strategy in Nizamabad district.

**Current custom hiring rates in Nizamabad district (April 2026):**

| Operation | Rate per Acre | NH 3230 TX Capacity | Daily Earning Potential |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rotavation | ₹900–1,100/acre | 5–6 acres/day | ₹4,500–6,600 |
| Disc ploughing | ₹1,000–1,200/acre | 4–5 acres/day | ₹4,000–6,000 |
| Transport | ₹600–800/trip | 4–6 trips/day | ₹2,400–4,800 |

A Balkonda small farmer with 10 acres can typically custom hire for **25–35 days/season** (over kharif and rabi):
- Custom hiring income: ₹4,500/day × 30 days = **₹1,35,000/season**
- Annual custom hiring income: ₹1.35 lakh (kharif) + ₹90,000 (rabi) = **₹2.25 lakh/year**
- Annual EMI burden: ₹11,000 × 12 = ₹1.32 lakh
- **Net surplus after EMI from custom hiring alone: ₹93,000/year**

This is the model followed by successful small-farm tractor owners in Balkonda, Yellareddy, and Bheemgal mandals — custom hiring more than covers the EMI.

### Subsidy Can Bring Entry Cost Down by ₹1–2 Lakh

For eligible farmers, subsidies significantly reduce the initial purchase cost:

- SC/ST farmers: Up to ₹1.5 lakh subsidy on purchase price
- Women farmers: 40–50% subsidy on purchase (max ₹1.25 lakh)
- Small/marginal farmers (below 5 acres): 25–40% under SMAM
- SHG members: NABARD-linked loans at 4–7% interest

After a ₹1.5 lakh SC/ST subsidy on an NH 3230 TX (₹6.8 lakh after GST and registration), the financed amount is ₹4.0–4.5 lakh. EMI at 9.5% for 60 months: **₹8,500–9,500/month** — very manageable even for small farms.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Is 35HP sufficient for paddy cultivation in Nizamabad's waterlogged conditions?**
For farms up to 15 acres with standard paddy cultivation (transplanting + rotavation + harvest transport), 35HP is fully sufficient. The NH 3230 TX's 1500 kg hydraulic lift handles standard paddy implements without difficulty. Only for very deep black cotton paddy soil or heavy 3-disc ploughing would you benefit from stepping up to 47HP.

**Q2: Can the NH 3230 TX operate a paddy transplanter?**
Yes, fully. The NH 3230 TX's PTO output (30 HP) comfortably operates paddy transplanters from major brands. This is one of the most common applications for the 3230 TX in Armoor and Dichpally mandals, where paddy transplanting area is expanding rapidly.

**Q3: How many acres can the NH 3230 TX rotavate in a single day?**
Under Nizamabad conditions (normal paddy field depth, 1.2m rotavator), the NH 3230 TX completes 5–6 acres per day on a full 8-hour working day. In custom hiring, this translates to ₹4,500–6,600 daily income at current market rates.

**Q4: What is the resale value of NH 3230 TX after 5 years?**
After 5 years of regular use, NH 3230 TX tractors in good condition sell for ₹3.8–4.5 lakh in the Nizamabad secondary market — approximately 55–65% of purchase price. This is significantly better than Swaraj 724 FE (35–45%) and comparable to Mahindra 265 DI.
