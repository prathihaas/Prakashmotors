## How Government Subsidy Brings NH 3230 Under ₹5.5 Lakh

Telangana farmers can access meaningful subsidies on new tractor purchases:

- **PM-KISAN Mechanisation Scheme**: 25–50% subsidy on tractor purchase for SC/ST and small/marginal farmers
- **Telangana State Agriculture Department**: Separate state-level subsidy of up to ₹75,000 for farmers with under 5 acres
- **NABARD Agri Finance**: Subsidised interest rates (as low as 7% effective) through cooperative banks

On a ₹6.75 lakh NH 3230 TX, combined subsidies can bring the effective cost to **₹5.2 – 5.5 lakh** for eligible buyers. Prakash Motors' documentation team assists with the full subsidy application process.

## Budget Tractor Buying Checklist for Telangana Farmers

Before you visit any showroom, use this checklist:

- [ ] **HP needs**: Is 35 HP sufficient for your biggest implement? (For rotavators over 7 feet: yes; for heavy subsoilers: consider 45+ HP)
- [ ] **Acreage**: Under 10 acres: 35 HP ideal. 10–20 acres: 35–40 HP fine. 20+ acres: consider 47 HP.
- [ ] **Crop mix**: Paddy + cotton + turmeric → prioritise hydraulics (NH 3230 wins)
- [ ] **Service access**: Can you reach the dealer within 30 minutes? Critical for harvest-time breakdowns.
- [ ] **Subsidy eligibility**: Check land holding, caste certificate, and farmer ID before purchase.
- [ ] **Resale horizon**: If selling in 5–7 years, buy New Holland for best resale value in the region.
