# Government Subsidies for Farm Machinery

The Telangana government has announced new subsidies for farm mechanization under the **Rythu Bandhu** related schemes.

## Eligibility
- Small and marginal farmers (owning less than 5 acres).
- SC/ST farmers are eligible for higher subsidy percentages.

## Machinery Covered
- Tractors (up to 50% subsidy)
- Paddy Transplanters
- Rotavators
- Harvesters

## How to Apply
Visit your local MeeSeva center or the Agriculture Officer in Nizamabad. You will need your Pattadar Passbook and Aadhar card.

**Contact Prakash Motors** for assistance with the quotation process required for loan applications.
