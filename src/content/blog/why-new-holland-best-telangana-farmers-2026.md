## Reason 2: Engineered for Tropical Heavy-Soil Farming

Not all tractor designs suit India's farming conditions. Many European or American brands are optimised for light, well-drained soils — not Telangana's deep black Vertisol clay, which expands when wet, contracts when dry, and demands maximum hydraulic force and torque during kharif operations.

New Holland specifically engineered its TX Plus and Excel series for South Asian tropical farming:

- **High-torque, low-RPM engine tuning**: Maximum torque at 1400–1600 RPM, not 2200+ RPM as in road-optimised engines — providing maximum pull at field working speeds
- **Turbocharger sized for tropical temperatures**: NH turbos are specified for continuous operation at 40–45°C ambient — common in Nizamabad's May–June pre-kharif period
- **Sealed electrical systems**: Designed for high-humidity paddy operations and dusty cotton field conditions
- **Aggressive traction tyre compatibility**: NH rear axle specs are optimised for 16.9x28 to 18.4x30 traction tyres used in Telangana conditions
