## Armoor's Agricultural Profile and Tractor Needs

Armoor mandal sits on a mix of red and black cotton soils. The northern portions closer to Balkonda see typical black clay soils ideal for paddy, while the southern reaches support chilli and turmeric cultivation on loamy red soils. This soil diversity means Armoor farmers need tractors that perform across a wide range of implements — from rotavators for paddy land prep to ridgers and bed formers for chilli nurseries.

## EMI Options for Armoor Tractor Buyers

Banks including SBI, Andhra Bank (now Union Bank), and Canara Bank offer tractor loans at competitive rates for Nizamabad district farmers. Armoor-area borrowers can apply at Nizamabad branch offices or through Prakash Motors' finance desk.

- Loan tenure: Up to 7 years
- Down payment: 15–25%
- Interest: 9–11% per annum

### NABARD Tractor Scheme
Under NABARD refinance, eligible Armoor farmers can access tractor loans at 7.5% interest through KCC (Kisan Credit Card) enhancement. This scheme particularly benefits small and marginal farmers.

### State Subsidy Eligibility
SC/ST farmers and women farmers in Armoor mandal are eligible for up to **50% subsidy** under SMAM scheme. General category farmers may receive **25% subsidy**, subject to government budget allocation. Prakash Motors assists with all subsidy applications free of charge.
