## Paddy-Specific Performance Analysis

### Hydraulic Lift — The Critical Metric for Paddy Cultivation

Paddy cultivation in Nizamabad involves several hydraulics-intensive operations:

**Puddling**: Cage wheels or peg-type puddlers in waterlogged fields require precise depth control and full implement retraction at field headlands. NH 3630's 1,700 kg lift vs JD 5050D's 1,500 kg gives the NH a 13% advantage in lifting force — directly reducing the "dragging" problem at turns.

**Paddy field levelling**: Laser-guided levellers (increasingly used in Nizamabad district) require precise hydraulic response. NH's open-centre live hydraulics provide smoother, more responsive control than the JD 5050D's system in this application.

**Post-paddy subsoiling**: After Kharif harvest, many Nizamabad farmers use a subsoiler to break the hard pan before Rabi. Soil resistance at this point is very high. NH's 1,700 kg lift + turbocharged engine handles this combination better than the naturally aspirated JD 5050D.
