## The Custom Hire Market in Nizamabad — Current Rates (2026)

Understanding the custom hire market is essential to the hire vs buy decision:

| Crop | Hire Rate (per acre) | Peak Season Availability | Wait Time (peak) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paddy (Kharif) | ₹1,500 – 2,200 | Oct 15 – Nov 15 | 7–14 days |
| Paddy (Rabi, limited) | ₹1,400 – 1,800 | Feb – Mar | 3–5 days |
| Wheat | ₹1,200 – 1,600 | Mar – Apr | 3–7 days |
| Maize | ₹1,800 – 2,500 | Dec – Jan | 5–10 days |

The harvest rate of ₹1,500–2,200 per acre in Kharif paddy season looks straightforward — until you factor in the cost of waiting. Paddy that should have been harvested on Day 60 post-transplant but is harvested on Day 74 (two weeks of queue) faces:
- Grain shattering loss: 4–7%
- Stalk lodging risk: increases significantly
- Fungal infection risk in humid October conditions: higher

On 20 acres of paddy at 25 quintals per acre, a 5% shattering loss = 25 quintals = approximately ₹31,250 at ₹1,250/quintal MSP. This hidden loss exceeds the cost of hiring a combine.
