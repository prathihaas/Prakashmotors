## Engine Performance — *ఇంజన్ పనితీరు*

The 4-cylinder turbocharged engine is the heart of the Workmaster 105. Its performance characteristics are notably different from the 3-cylinder engines in New Holland's smaller tractors:

**Torque reserve**: The 4-cylinder configuration provides exceptional torque backup — when the engine encounters resistance (hard soil, heavy implement, slope), it has substantial reserve to maintain working speed without gear-down.

**High-altitude and high-temperature performance**: In Telangana's summer months, when ambient temperatures regularly exceed 40°C, turbocharged engines can lose significant power. The Workmaster 105's larger displacement and intercooling technology (where fitted) maintains better power stability in extreme heat.

**Noise and vibration**: The 4-cylinder engine provides notably smoother vibration characteristics than 3-cylinder engines — important for operator comfort during long working days.

**Fuel consumption**: At 100HP+ workload, expect 8–12 litres per hour. This is significant — at ₹100/litre, that's ₹800–₹1,200/hour in fuel costs. High work rates (acres per hour) are essential to justify this.

## Hydraulic System — *హైడ్రాలిక్ వ్యవస్థ*

The 3500–4000 kg hydraulic lifting capacity is the standout figure. This enables:
- Very wide implements (8–12 ft rotavators, heavy disc ploughs)
- Three-point mounted planters and seeders for large operations
- Heavy dozer blades and land leveling equipment
- Large bale wrappers and handling equipment

Hydraulic flow at 61 L/min is sufficient for demanding implements that require continuous hydraulic operation (side-shift discs, precision planting equipment).

## Gearbox — 12F + 3R

The 12-speed forward gearbox gives the Workmaster 105 exceptional versatility:
- True creep speeds for precision operations (transplanting, precision seeding)
- Full transport speeds for road travel
- Multiple field working speeds to match different implements

For large farm operations where the tractor moves between different tasks throughout the day — ploughing in the morning, transport at midday, seeding in the afternoon — this flexibility is valuable.

## Real-World Performance in Telangana Operations

### Land Leveling
Large farm consolidations, new land development in Nizamabad district's expansion areas, and leveling for drip irrigation systems are common applications. The Workmaster 105 with a rear blade or dozer attachment handles these efficiently.

### Deep Tillage / Subsoiling
For large sugarcane operations in the Bodhan area, breaking deep compaction (30+ cm) requires serious power. The Workmaster 105 with a 5–7 tyne subsoiler covers large areas efficiently — critical for sugarcane yield optimization.

### Wide Rotavation
A 9–10 ft rotavator on a 105HP tractor covers dramatically more area per hour than smaller tractors. For a 200-acre operation needing rotavation before sowing, the time savings are enormous.

### Large Paddy Operations
For commercial paddy cultivation across large areas, the Workmaster 105 with wide implements finishes land preparation days earlier than smaller tractors — potentially capturing better transplanting windows.

Comparison with a 47HP tractor doing the same rotavation:
- 47HP: 3.0–3.5 L/hr, 1.5–2 acres/hr → 1.5–2.0 L/acre
- 105HP: 8–10 L/hr, 4–5 acres/hr → 1.8–2.2 L/acre

The 105HP uses more total fuel but covers more area, so per-acre cost is not dramatically different. The real advantage is time — completing field work faster.
