## Kamareddy Soil Profile — Why It Matters for Tractor Choice

Understanding your soil is the first step to choosing the right tractor. Kamareddy district features two major soil zones:

**Black Cotton Soil (Western Kamareddy)**
Found in mandals like Banswada, Yellareddy, and Bhiknoor, this heavy clay soil swells when wet and cracks when dry. It requires:
- Minimum 45 HP for deep primary tillage
- High hydraulic lift capacity (1,600 kg+)
- Heavy-duty transmission for sustained heavy draught

**Red & Sandy Loam Soil (Eastern Kamareddy)**
Found near Kamareddy town, Ramareddy, and Raikal mandals, this soil supports cotton and maize with lighter implements. Here, 35–47 HP tractors perform excellently.
