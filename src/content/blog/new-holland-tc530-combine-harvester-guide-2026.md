## Crops Suited to TC5.30 in Telangana

The TC5.30 handles all major Nizamabad Kharif and Rabi crops:

- **Paddy (Kharif)** — Primary use; handles wet and lodged paddy with low grain loss
- **Wheat (Rabi)** — Highly efficient with wheat header attachment
- **Maize** — With maize header (optional attachment); growing demand in Yellareddy and Banswada
- **Soybean** — Increasingly grown in Bodhan and Balkonda mandals

The Transverse Axial Flow threshing system is particularly well-suited to Telangana's paddy varieties including BPT 5204 and Telangana Sona, which tend to have fragile grain heads. Conventional drum threshers cause high breakage; the TAF system is gentler and reduces grain loss to under 1.5%.
