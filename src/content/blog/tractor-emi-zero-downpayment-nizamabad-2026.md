## Finance Options Available at Prakash Motors

### 1. New Holland Finance (CNH Capital)

NH Finance is the in-house financing arm of CNH Industrial and offers the most flexible terms:
- Loan-to-value (LTV): up to 100% of on-road price (zero down payment)
- Tenure: 36 to 84 months
- Interest rate: 8.5–10.5% p.a. (depends on tenure and credit profile)
- Processing time: 3–5 business days
- Special schemes: lower interest rates for women farmers, SC/ST applicants

### 2. State Bank of India (SBI) Kisan Credit Scheme

SBI is the most widely used bank for tractor finance among Nizamabad farmers:
- Up to 90% LTV (10% down payment standard, zero DP for select cases)
- Interest rate: 8.5–9.5% (with government interest subvention, effective rate can be 7.5%)
- Linked to PM-KISAN — existing KCC holders get faster approval
- Repayment aligned with crop cycles on request

### 3. HDFC Bank Agri Loan

- Up to 90% LTV
- Competitive rates: 9.0–10.0% p.a.
- Fast digital verification for applicants with existing HDFC accounts
- Doorstep document collection available in Nizamabad

### 4. Axis Bank Farm Loan

- Up to 85% LTV
- Flexible EMI holidays (3-month moratorium at start)
- Good option for farmers with existing Axis savings accounts

## How Rythu Bandhu Offsets Your Tractor EMI

Telangana's Rythu Bandhu scheme provides ₹5,000 per acre per season (Kharif + Rabi = ₹10,000 per acre per year) directly to eligible farmers. Here is how that intersects with tractor finance:

| Farm Size | Annual Rythu Bandhu | Monthly Equivalent | EMI Coverage |
|---|---|---|---|
| 5 acres | ₹50,000 | ₹4,167 | 28% of NH 3230 TX EMI |
| 10 acres | ₹1,00,000 | ₹8,333 | 50% of NH 3630 TX EMI |
| 15 acres | ₹1,50,000 | ₹12,500 | 76% of NH 3630 TX EMI |
| 20 acres | ₹2,00,000 | ₹16,667 | Covers NH 3630 TX fully |

A 20-acre farmer receiving Rythu Bandhu can effectively finance a NH 3630 TX Plus entirely from the scheme's disbursements, with crop income remaining untouched for other farm expenses.

## PM-KISAN Installment as EMI Top-Up

PM-KISAN provides ₹6,000 per year (₹2,000 per installment) to all eligible small and marginal farmers. While this alone does not cover an EMI, it supplements Rythu Bandhu and can cover 1–2 months of EMI per year, reducing the effective cash burden.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I get a New Holland tractor loan with zero down payment in Nizamabad?**
A: Yes. New Holland Finance (NH Finance) offers up to 100% loan-to-value for eligible applicants, meaning zero rupee down payment. SBI and HDFC also offer zero to 10% down payment options. Eligibility depends on credit profile, land holding, and income assessment.

**Q: What documents do I need for a tractor loan at Prakash Motors?**
A: Core documents: Aadhaar card, 7/12 Pahani (land records from Dharani portal), 6-month bank passbook, recent passport photos, and crop sale receipts as income proof. Prakash Motors assists with document compilation and bank submission.

**Q: What is the interest rate on New Holland tractor loans in 2026?**
A: Interest rates range from 8.5% (NH Finance, short tenure, excellent credit) to 10.5% p.a. (standard commercial rates). With SBI's government interest subvention scheme for farm mechanisation, effective rates for eligible farmers can be as low as 7.5% p.a.

**Q: Can I use Rythu Bandhu money as down payment?**
A: Yes. Rythu Bandhu disbursements are credited to your linked bank account and can be used as down payment if you prefer a lower EMI over the zero down payment option. A 15-acre farmer receiving ₹1.5 lakh annually can use one full year's Rythu Bandhu as down payment, reducing monthly EMI significantly.
