## Engine Performance — *ఇంజన్ పనితీరు*

The 3630 TX Plus runs a 3-cylinder turbocharged diesel engine producing 47 horsepower. What really stands out is how this engine performs at lower RPMs. In Nizamabad's black cotton soil — especially during kharif season paddy cultivation — you need consistent torque at low speeds. The 3630 TX Plus delivers exactly that.

During rotavation of clay-heavy soil near Dharpally and Nizamsagar mandal areas, farmers have reported the engine pulling without strain even at 1400 RPM. That's a significant advantage over some competitor tractors that lug at the same conditions.

**Fuel economy**: Expect 2.8 to 3.2 litres per hour under heavy load during cultivation. For transport work, fuel consumption drops to 1.8–2.2 litres per hour. Over a full kharif season, this translates to meaningful savings compared to older tractors in the same class.

---

## Gearbox & Transmission — *గేర్‌బాక్స్*

The 8 forward + 2 reverse synchromesh gearbox is smooth and predictable. Gear changes under load are not jerky — a complaint common with some budget tractor brands. The clutch is well-calibrated for Nizamabad's field conditions where you frequently shift between transport speed and working speed.

For paddy transplanting operations, the lower gears provide creep speeds essential for precision. For cotton field operations near Banswada, the mid-range gears handle deep tillage without complaint.

---

## Hydraulics & Lifting Capacity — *హైడ్రాలిక్స్*

1500 kg lifting capacity is the headline number, but what matters more is the consistent pressure delivery. The draft control and position control system on the 3630 TX Plus is responsive. Rotavators, disc ploughs, and MB ploughs mount and operate without hunting or sudden drops.

For farmers using multi-crop implements across cotton, turmeric, and paddy in Kamareddy and Bheemgal areas, this matters enormously. The implement stays at consistent depth without constant manual correction.

## Field Performance in Nizamabad Conditions

### Paddy Operations
For paddy (kharif) cultivation — the dominant crop in Nizamabad district — the 3630 TX Plus handles:
- Puddling: Excellent. Handles 3-4 depth in flooded conditions
- Transplanter operation: Smooth, consistent speed control
- Post-harvest field preparation: Paired with rotavator, completes 1 acre in under 2 hours

### Cotton Cultivation
Cotton fields in Banswada and Yellareddy areas often have harder, drier soil before the first rains. The 3630 TX Plus with a 3-bottom disc plough handles this well, though for very hard pan soils you may need 2 passes.

### Turmeric Farming
Turmeric requires deep bed preparation — often 12-15 inches. Paired with a ridger, the 3630 TX Plus manages turmeric beds in Nizamabad's red laterite soil without straining the hydraulics.
