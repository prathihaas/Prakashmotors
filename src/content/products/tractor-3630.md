# The All-Rounder for Indian Agriculture

The **New Holland 3630** is the most popular tractor in its segment. It combines heritage with modern technology to deliver exceptional performance.

## Engine Power
Powered by a robust engine that delivers high torque even at low RPMs, ensuring you never run out of power during deep ploughing.

## Versatility
Whether it's rotavator, cultivator, or haulage, the 3630 handles everything with ease. The hydraulics are sensitive and precise, perfect for leveling operations.

## Comfort
Designed with the operator in mind, it features a flat platform, side-shift gears, and a comfortable seat for long working hours.
