# The Multi-Crop Champion

The **New Holland TC5.30** is a powerhouse combine harvester designed for Indian farming conditions. With a massive 130 HP engine, it delivers exceptional performance across multiple crops, ensuring maximum grain recovery and minimal loss.

## Rotary Separator Technology
Unlike standard harvesters, the TC5.30 features a unique Rotary Separator. This additional threshing mechanism ensures that even in damp or high-yield crops, grain separation is thorough, reducing wastage significantly.

## True Multi-Crop Versatility
This machine is not just for paddy. It is engineered to harvest a wide variety of crops including Maize, Soybean, Wheat, and Pulses with minimal adjustments, making it a profitable investment for contractors.

The advanced cleaning system ensures that the harvested grain is clean and whole. With double drum threshing technology, it separates grain gently but effectively, fetching you a better market price.
