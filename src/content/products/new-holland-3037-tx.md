# The Multi-Crop Master

The **New Holland 3037 TX** is a robust 39 HP tractor designed to perform equally well in soil preparation and haulage. It is a true multi-purpose machine.

## Eptraa PTO
The advanced PTO system allows for multiple speed options, making it compatible with a wide range of implements, from water pumps to threshers.

## Built for Haulage
With high ground clearance and a powerful engine, the 3037 TX handles fully loaded trolleys with ease, even on rough village roads.

## Operator Comfort
Features smooth steering and an ergonomic seat arrangement, reducing driver stress during long days of ploughing or transport.
